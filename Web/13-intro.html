Indian Case Challenge 2013

Indian Case Challenge saw its second edition in 2013. This time, bigger and better, ICC 2012, got registrations of over 500 teams for the preliminary round.

The teams were tested first with a pre-case given to them. The final scrutinization was based on their case solution and we had top 10 teams competing with each other on a real-world business case scenario.

An excellent amalgam of business knowledge, tough competition, exciting prizes and fun, ICC 2013 was certainly one of the best case-study competitions in India.
