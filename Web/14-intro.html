Indian Case Challenge 2014

It is a National Case Study Competition, organized by the students of the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, it challenges the best Undergraduate and Graduate students from all over India to compete against each other and prove their business and entrepreneurial skills in a real life business case scenario. Not only business skills and technical understanding are necessary, but also the feeling for entrepreneurship and a sense for market opportunities is needed.

The Indian Case Challenge, popularly known as ICC, during the month of January every year. The ICC shall also give you the platform for making business contacts, bringing future leaders and business masters together. The organizational team of ICC, i.e. B-club, tries to combine this competitive event with a great fresh atmosphere for national exchange and friendship.

Come to Kharagpur and challenge the best!
