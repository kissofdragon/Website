BClub, IIT Kharagpur

Welcome to B-Club. We are IIT Kharagpur’s in-campus student society for all things related to business. The club’s primary objective is to provide for students a non-profit organisation, where they can improve their understanding of subjects such as finance, consulting, business strategy, data analytics and entrepreneurship. We provide our members with a knowledge sharing interface, where they are able to interact with each other and also with teachers, alumni and industry professionals. Besides, we also conduct educational workshops, seminars with eminent personalities, debates and group discussions. Although it’s been only a few years, the club has come very far already. This is evident from the fact that it has been the budding ground for several start-ups and also mentored many winners of major business related competitions across the country.

How does Business Club works?

At B-Club, members learn by interacting with the mentors and discussing latest topics in all of the above fields on a bi-weekly basis. Our motto is 'To Learn and Develop'. Club members eagerly look forward to these meetings, where they learn and share ideas and strategies from the world of business and academia.
