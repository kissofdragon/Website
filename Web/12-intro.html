Indian Case Challenge 2012

The Indian Case Challenge(ICC) 2012, presented itself as the first-of-its-kind event from B-Club, IIT Kharagpur which involved the brightest business-savvy minds studying across various undergraduate and post graduate levels in all the B and Tech. Schools and Universities in India to solve real-world business case studies. In its first year of conception, ICC saw over 300 teams registering for the event which was an ace-start for a streak of events from the club.
