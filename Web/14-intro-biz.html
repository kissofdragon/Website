Indian Biz Challenge 2014

“We keep moving forward, opening new doors, and doing new things, because we're curious and curiosity keeps leading us down new paths.”

Today’s business world likes to focus on innovating in your industry and using that innovation to develop sustained business advantage in the market.

B-Club, IIT Kharagpur announces the first edition of its B-Plan competition, the Indian Biz Challenge (IBC), as a sister event of ICC, exclusively for IIT Kharagpur, hosted in collaboration with the venture capitalist “Saif Partners”.

IBC provides the innovators of IIT Kharagpur with a platform to interact with like-minded colleagues and also pitch their ideas before a leading venture capitalist of India and stand a chance to win a great opportunity to get started.
